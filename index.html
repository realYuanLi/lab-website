Biostatistics · Perelman School of Medicine

Statistical methodology for longitudinal health data.

The Huang Lab develops statistical methods for multivariate longitudinal data to support risk prediction, disease-trajectory characterization, and the integration of evidence across registries, hospitals, and claims systems.

Research

All research areas →

    Multivariate, functional, and joint models for irregular health trajectories.

    Integrating registries, claims, and EHRs to forecast outcomes in pediatric disease.

  • 05

    Mobile health

    Adaptive decision rules from streams of patient-generated data and wearables.

    County-level transmission modelling, with CHOP PolicyLab.
