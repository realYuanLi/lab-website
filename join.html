Join Us

Join the lab

We welcome inquiries from prospective postdoctoral fellows, PhD and Masters students, and research assistants interested in biostatistics for longitudinal health data.

Who we look for

Strong candidates have a background in statistics, biostatistics, computer science, or a closely related quantitative field. Familiarity with longitudinal data, EHR or claims data, causal inference, or Bayesian methods is helpful but not required. We particularly value scientific curiosity, careful writing, and an interest in real clinical problems.

Open positions

Postdoctoral fellows

One to two postdoctoral positions are typically open each year. Projects can be aligned with any of the lab's research areas; we will work with you to shape the direction during the first months.

To apply: email a CV, a short research statement (1–2 pages), one or two representative papers (if available), and the names of three references.

PhD students

PhD students join the lab through Penn's graduate programmes. Please apply through the Department of Biostatistics or the Biomedical Graduate Studies programme and mention the lab in your statement of purpose. Admitted students are welcome to email and arrange a meeting.

Masters students

Masters students based in Biostatistics or Epidemiology & Informatics at Penn are welcome to reach out once admitted. We typically support one or two Masters projects at a time.

Research assistants & undergraduates

A small number of research-assistant projects are available each semester. Email a CV and a brief note describing your interests and availability.
